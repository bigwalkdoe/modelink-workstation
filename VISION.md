# Modelink Workstation v1.0

## AI-Native Engineering Platform

**Codename:** Atlas

# Vision

**Modelink Workstation** is not a Linux distribution for general consumers. It is a purpose-built engineering workstation designed for AI engineers, SaaS architects, Linux administrators, DevOps engineers, cybersecurity professionals, and technical founders.

Its primary objective is to eliminate development environment setup by providing a fully integrated, reproducible, enterprise-grade engineering platform from first boot.

> **"From Power On to Productive in Under 10 Minutes."**

---

# Core Philosophy

Modelink Workstation follows seven foundational principles:

1. AI-first
2. Developer-first
3. Container-first
4. Infrastructure-as-Code
5. Secure-by-default
6. Automation-first
7. Enterprise-ready

Every system component should support these principles.
